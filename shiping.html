Shipping Policy

Last updated: 22 Aug 2025
Made-to-order: Most Zenvora products are custom-printed after you place the order. Processing time is separate from courier transit time.

1) Processing & Dispatch

Orders are processed Mon–Sat (excluding public holidays). Standard processing time is 1–3 business days for most items. Bulk/complex customizations may take 3–6 business days.

2) Estimated Delivery Timelines (India)

Region Metro Non‑metro / Tier‑2/3
North, West, South 2–4 business days 3–6 business days
East & North‑East 3–6 business days 5–9 business days

Transit timelines begin after dispatch. Remote locations & weather disruptions can extend delivery times.

3) Shipping Fees

  • Standard shipping: ₹59–₹99 (auto‑calculated at checkout by weight/pincode).
  • Free shipping: orders above ₹999 (India only) unless otherwise stated.
  • Express shipping: Shown at checkout where available.

4) Order Tracking

Once dispatched, you’ll receive an email/SMS with your tracking link. You can also check status from My Orders in your Zenvora account.

5) Address Changes

Address edits are possible before dispatch only. Contact us quickly with your Order ID.

6) Delivery Attempts

Couriers usually make 2 attempts. If undelivered, the package may be returned. Re‑shipping charges may apply.

7) Cash on Delivery (COD)

  • COD availability varies by pincode and order value.
  • Please keep exact cash ready; open‑box/try‑and‑buy is not supported.

8) Damages, Missing or Lost Packages

Inspect your order upon delivery. If an item is defective, damaged, or if you receive the wrong item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with unboxing photos/videos so we can evaluate and make it right.

9) International Shipping

  • Currently limited. Duties/taxes (if any) are payable by the recipient.
  • Transit estimate: 7–15 business days after dispatch.

10) Holiday & Force Majeure Delays

Expect additional delays during festivals, sales, or events beyond our control (weather, strikes, local restrictions).

11) Multiple Shipments

Items in the same order may ship separately to speed up delivery.

Heads up: Delivery timelines are estimates provided by courier partners and not guaranteed. We’ll always do our best to help if things run late.

FAQs

How do I track my order?

Use the tracking link shared over SMS/email after dispatch or visit My Orders when logged in.

Can I change my address after placing the order?

Yes, if the order hasn’t been dispatched. Contact support with your Order ID immediately.

Do you offer same‑day or next‑day delivery?

Express options may appear at checkout based on your pincode and item type.

What if my package is marked delivered but I haven’t received it?

Please check with security/neighbor, then contact us within 24 hours so we can raise a ticket with the courier.
